# Welcome to Discolytics!

- Discolytics is an open source platform to monitor key health and performance metrics of your Discord bot.
- Discolytics automatically monitors your bot and immediatelly calls, texts, and delivers e-mail alerts to notify your team of downtime.
- All repositories are open source, including the dashboard, several language specific libraries, and alert monitoring.

You are free to download, modify, and implement Discolytics as you need for your organization.
